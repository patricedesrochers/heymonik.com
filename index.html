How It Works

Three steps. Zero coding.

1
🧠

Turn on the Brain

Click one button and work normally. Monik watches your screen — capturing screenshots at every click and keystroke — building a visual timeline of your workflow.

2
⚡

It Learns Your Tasks

After seeing you do a task a few times, Monik identifies the pattern, extracts the steps, and compiles them into an executable task. Or click Teach to show it once.

3
🤖

Walk Away

Leave your desk. Monik detects you're gone, switches to autonomous mode, and starts running your learned tasks. Come back to find the work done and documented.
